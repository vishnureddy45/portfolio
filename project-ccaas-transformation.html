CCaaS Contact Center Transformation

40–50%Self-Service Containment
15–25%Reduction in Live-Agent Demand
10–25%Cost Per Contact Reduction
3–5%Abandonment Rate

Led the end-to-end CCaaS transformation for a major enterprise client, migrating from a legacy on-premise contact center to Amazon Connect — enabling a modern, AI-powered, cloud-native customer engagement platform that scaled across voice and digital channels simultaneously.

Designed the full omnichannel customer journey architecture — covering inbound voice, outbound campaigns, web chat and digital messaging — with intelligent routing logic that used intent, customer segment and sentiment signals to connect customers to the right resource at the right time.

Integrated Amazon Connect with Salesforce CRM and Workforce Management (WFM) systems, giving agents a unified desktop view of customer history, open cases and real-time interaction data — reducing average handle time and improving the quality of every customer conversation.

Implemented virtual assistant and chatbot solutions using Amazon Lex, enabling self-service for high-frequency intents and achieving 40–50% containment — significantly reducing live-agent demand while maintaining CSAT scores above target.

Delivered 10–25% reduction in cost per contact through channel shift, automation, AHT/ACW optimisation and deflection of routine requests — with abandonment rates reduced to 3–5% through improved queue design, forecasting and callback strategies.

Supported multiple RFP responses and client pursuits with technical leadership, solution architecture and commercial inputs — directly contributing to deal wins and accelerating the client's digital transformation roadmap.
