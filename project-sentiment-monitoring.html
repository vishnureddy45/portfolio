AI-Driven Social Sentiment Monitoring

ProactiveCX Intervention
High-ImpactCustomer Identification
Real-TimeSentiment Signals
ImprovedBrand Sentiment

Built an ML-based system to identify high-impact customers generating negative social sentiment about the brand — enabling the CX team to proactively engage with detractors before their sentiment amplified, turning potential brand damage into recovery opportunities.

Designed and implemented a multi-source data ingestion pipeline using legal web scraping to aggregate social media posts, reviews and public forum discussions at scale — feeding a real-time analytics engine that processed thousands of data points daily.

Developed the influence scoring model using NLP and machine learning — combining sentiment classification, reach metrics and engagement signals to rank customers by their potential impact on brand perception, enabling the CX team to prioritise outreach to the highest-risk individuals first.

Applied advanced NLP techniques including named entity recognition, topic modelling and aspect-based sentiment analysis to extract granular insights from unstructured text — identifying not just that a customer was unhappy, but specifically what product, service or interaction had caused the negative sentiment.

Deployed the solution on AWS with Elasticsearch for fast, scalable search and retrieval of customer profiles and sentiment trends — giving the CX team a real-time dashboard that surfaced actionable intelligence rather than raw data, enabling them to act within hours rather than days.

The system directly improved customer retention by enabling personalised, proactive outreach to at-risk customers — demonstrating that AI-driven social intelligence could be a genuine competitive advantage in customer experience management.
