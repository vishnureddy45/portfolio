Amazon Q Self-Service Enablement

40–70%Fewer Zero-Result Searches
20–60sAHT Saving Per Interaction
75–90%Search Success Rate
GovernedKnowledge Management

Implemented Amazon Q-based self-service capabilities to deliver intelligent, contextual responses for customer queries — moving beyond keyword-matching search to genuine generative AI-powered knowledge retrieval that understood the intent behind a question, not just the words used.

Designed the knowledge governance framework — curating, structuring and maintaining the knowledge base that Amazon Q drew from, ensuring responses were accurate, up-to-date and aligned with the organisation's policies and compliance requirements.

Reduced zero-result searches by 40–70% by replacing a legacy FAQ system with Amazon Q's semantic understanding — customers who previously hit dead ends now received relevant, contextual answers even when their query didn't match exact keyword patterns.

Integrated Amazon Q directly into the Amazon Connect contact flow, enabling the self-service layer to surface knowledge base answers during IVR interactions and chat sessions — saving 20–60 seconds of AHT per interaction by eliminating agent knowledge lookup time.

Improved overall search success rate to 75–90%, measured through continuous monitoring of query resolution rates, customer satisfaction signals and escalation patterns — with iterative knowledge base improvements driven by real usage data rather than assumptions.
