Real-Time Contact Center Analytics

Wipro Technologies BFSI Amazon Connect, Amazon Transcribe, Amazon Comprehend, AWS Lambda, Python, Azure

Designed and implemented a cloud-integrated contact center analytics platform on Amazon Connect, delivering real-time voice AI capabilities — including live transcription, sentiment analysis and supervisor dashboards — that transformed how the client's support teams monitored and improved customer interactions.

Integrated Amazon Transcribe for real-time call transcription, enabling supervisors to monitor live conversations, identify escalation risks and intervene proactively — reducing customer churn and improving resolution quality across the contact center.

Deployed Amazon Comprehend for real-time sentiment analysis, surfacing customer emotion signals during active calls and providing agents with live coaching prompts to de-escalate tense interactions before they became complaints.

Built a real-time supervisor dashboard aggregating live call metrics, sentiment trends and agent performance data — giving contact center managers unprecedented visibility into the customer experience as it happens.

Architected the solution to support both AWS and Azure environments using open-source tooling, ensuring flexibility and avoiding vendor lock-in while maintaining enterprise-grade reliability and performance at scale.
