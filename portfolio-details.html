CCaaS Contact Center Transformation

Accenture Insurance StateFarm Insurance, US Amazon Connect, Lex, Lambda, Salesforce, Transcribe, Comprehend, Polly, DynamoDB

Architected and delivered a full-scale Contact Center as a Service (CCaaS) transformation for a major US insurance provider, migrating from a legacy on-premise system to Amazon Connect — enabling a modern, AI-powered omnichannel customer experience platform that dramatically improved agent efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Designed the end-to-end customer journey across voice and chat channels, incorporating intelligent routing, real-time data lookups and personalised IVR flows that reduced average handle time and improved first-call resolution rates significantly.

Integrated Amazon Connect with Salesforce CRM, Workforce Management (WFM) and AWS AI services including Amazon Lex, Transcribe, Comprehend and Polly — creating a unified, data-driven contact center ecosystem that gave agents and supervisors unprecedented visibility into every customer interaction.

Built and deployed backend Lambda APIs integrated directly into Connect flows, enabling real-time customer data retrieval, dynamic personalisation and intelligent escalation logic that adapted to each caller's context and history.

Developed compelling POC demos and technical assets for C-suite stakeholders, showcasing the business value of AI-driven CCaaS capabilities and accelerating executive buy-in for the full transformation programme.

Supported multiple RFP responses with compelling technical narratives and solution architectures, directly contributing to new business wins in the Insurance vertical.
