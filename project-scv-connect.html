SCV + Amazon Connect Voice Integration

EMEA · NAMER · APACMulti-Region Delivery
CI/CDAutomated Releases
Dynamic Caller IDOutbound Personalisation
Zero-DowntimeMulti-Region Deployments

Delivered enterprise-grade voice integration between Salesforce Service Cloud Voice (SCV) and Amazon Connect — architecting a solution that gave agents a fully embedded telephony experience within Salesforce while leveraging the full power of Amazon Connect's AI and routing capabilities.

Designed and implemented advanced contact flows with Dynamic Language Prompts — enabling the IVR to adapt its language, tone and content dynamically based on customer profile data retrieved in real time from backend systems via Lambda integrations.

Implemented outbound Dynamic Caller ID, allowing the contact center to present contextually relevant phone numbers to customers based on their region, product line or relationship — improving answer rates and brand recognition across outbound campaigns.

Architected and deployed the solution across EMEA, NAMER and APAC regions, building CI/CD pipelines that automated the deployment of Amazon Connect configurations, Lambda functions and contact flow updates — bringing real consistency and speed to multi-region releases with zero-downtime deployments.

Built comprehensive monitoring and alerting using CloudWatch, providing real-time visibility into contact flow performance, Lambda execution metrics and integration health — enabling proactive issue detection and rapid resolution across all regions.
