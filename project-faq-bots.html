Self-Service FAQ Bots

88–94%Intent Accuracy
4.3–4.7/5Bot CSAT
30–60%Reduced Handoff Re-asks
High-VolumeCall Deflection

Designed and implemented AI-powered self-service FAQ bots to deflect high-frequency, routine customer queries away from live agents — enabling customers to get accurate, instant answers 24/7 without waiting in queue or speaking to an agent.

Built the NLP layer using Amazon Lex with carefully engineered intents, utterance training data and slot-filling logic — enabling the bot to handle natural, conversational queries rather than requiring customers to use specific keywords or phrases.

Achieved 88–94% intent accuracy through a rigorous continuous improvement cycle: monitoring misclassified utterances, analysing fallback patterns, retraining intent models and tuning confidence thresholds — delivering a bot experience that genuinely felt intelligent rather than frustrating.

Integrated the bots with backend systems via Lambda to provide personalised, data-driven responses — pulling live account information, policy details and transaction history to answer queries that previously required agent lookup and manual response.

Implemented seamless live-agent escalation with full context handoff — when the bot reached its limits, agents received the complete conversation history, identified intent and customer data, eliminating the need for customers to repeat themselves and reducing re-ask rates by 30–60%.

Delivered bot CSAT scores of 4.3–4.7/5 — a result of continuous evaluation, user feedback analysis and iterative tuning that kept the bot experience aligned with real customer expectations rather than just technical benchmarks.
