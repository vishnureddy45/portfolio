GenAI Predictive Queue Intent — Omnichannel Routing

DynamicAI Routing
ReducedMisroutes
ImprovedFCR
Voice + ChatOmnichannel

Built an AI-driven Predictive Queue Intent solution that replaced static, rule-based routing with dynamic, GenAI-powered intent classification — enabling Amazon Connect to intelligently route both voice and chat interactions based on real-time understanding of customer intent, not just DNIS or menu selections.

Leveraged AWS Bedrock and queue intent descriptions to train and deploy a GenAI model that analyses customer utterances, predicts the most likely service need, and routes interactions to the optimal queue or agent group — reducing misroutes and improving first-contact resolution across all channels.

Designed the solution to work across omnichannel touchpoints — voice IVR, web chat and digital messaging — with a unified intent model that ensured consistent routing logic regardless of the channel a customer chose to engage through.

Integrated the predictive routing engine directly into Amazon Connect contact flows via Lambda, enabling real-time intent scoring with sub-second latency — ensuring no degradation in customer experience while dramatically improving routing accuracy.

The solution eliminated the need for customers to navigate complex menu trees, instead allowing them to state their need naturally and be connected to the right resource immediately — a fundamental shift in how the contact center handled inbound demand.
