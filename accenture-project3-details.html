Intelligent Conversational IVR

Accenture Insurance StateFarm Insurance, US Amazon Connect, Amazon Lex, AWS Lambda, DynamoDB, CloudWatch, S3, Amazon Polly

Designed and deployed a next-generation Conversational IVR system on Amazon Connect, replacing legacy DTMF touch-tone menus with natural language understanding — enabling customers to speak naturally and be routed intelligently, dramatically improving self-service rates and customer satisfaction scores.

Integrated Amazon Lex for advanced NLU capabilities, enabling the IVR to accurately interpret caller intent, handle multi-turn conversations and dynamically route calls based on context — eliminating the frustration of rigid, hierarchical menu trees.

Developed dynamic, line-of-business-specific IVR flows tailored to different product and service categories, ensuring customers reach the right team faster and with greater accuracy — reducing misrouted calls and repeat contacts.

Leveraged AWS Lambda for real-time data retrieval during IVR interactions — surfacing live account information, policy status and personalised prompts to create a context-aware, human-like caller experience.

Implemented intelligent escalation paths that seamlessly transition callers from self-service to live agents, preserving full conversation context via DynamoDB session storage for a frictionless, zero-repeat handoff.

Delivered measurable business impact: increased call containment rates, reduced average handle time and improved customer effort scores through intelligent automation and personalised voice experiences.
