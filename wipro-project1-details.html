Voice of the Customer AI

Wipro Technologies BFSI Capital One, Richmond Innovation Center, USA Python, R, Machine Learning, NLP, AWS, Elasticsearch, Sentiment Analysis

Developed and deployed an NLP-driven machine learning solution to identify high-impact customers generating significant brand noise on social media — enabling the client to proactively engage with key influencers and transform negative sentiment into positive customer experiences.

Built a multi-stage ML pipeline combining social media data ingestion, NLP-based sentiment analysis and influence scoring models to surface the customers most likely to amplify brand perception — giving the CX team a prioritised engagement queue updated in near real-time.

Applied advanced NLP techniques including named entity recognition, topic modelling and sentiment classification using Python and AWS services, achieving high accuracy in identifying actionable customer signals from unstructured social data at scale.

Implemented legal web scraping pipelines to aggregate social media data at scale, feeding a real-time analytics engine that provided the client's CX team with prioritised customer engagement queues.

Deployed the solution on AWS with Elasticsearch for fast, scalable search and retrieval of customer profiles and sentiment trends — enabling the support team to act on insights in near real-time rather than days later.

Led proof-of-concept initiatives that demonstrated measurable ROI, directly influencing the client's decision to scale the solution across additional markets and social platforms.
