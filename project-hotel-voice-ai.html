Hotel Reservation Voice AI POC

Real-TimeSpeech-to-Speech
Zero-AgentSelf-Service
NaturalConversational Flow
Multi-TurnDialogue

Developed a voice-based hotel reservation Proof of Concept using AWS Nova Sonic's real-time speech-to-speech capabilities — enabling natural, human-like customer conversations for booking, modification and cancellation without any agent involvement or pre-recorded prompts.

This was a genuinely novel implementation: rather than using traditional TTS/ASR pipelines with scripted responses, the solution used Nova Sonic's end-to-end speech model to generate contextually appropriate, natural-sounding voice responses in real time — creating a conversation that felt like speaking to a knowledgeable human assistant.

Designed the multi-turn dialogue management layer to handle the full reservation workflow — capturing guest preferences, checking availability, confirming bookings and handling modifications — all through natural conversation without requiring customers to navigate menus or repeat information.

Integrated the voice AI layer with Amazon Connect to handle telephony, call routing and session management — enabling the POC to run as a fully functional phone-based service that could be demonstrated to clients in a live environment.

The POC directly influenced client decisions on AI-powered self-service strategy, demonstrating that voice AI had matured to the point where it could handle complex, multi-step service interactions with a level of naturalness that legacy IVR systems could never achieve.
